Festival Rules

Entry Rules
  • Possible categories are action, drama, documentary, animation, comedy, and commercials

  • Your movie may contain any tasteful subject.

  • Films may be no longer than six minutes including credits.

  • Remember this is a school event appealing to a wide audience. There should be no swearing or nudity in the films.

  • Students are to respect all laws when filming their movies. (refrain from using jackass style stunts, please)

  • No people or animals are to be harmed for the movies.

  • All videos will be screened by entry judges to make sure no rules are being broken

  • Films must be original material and contain only Aberhart students as cast and crew (with the exception of documentaries).

  • Films must be submitted in one of the following formats: VHS tape, CD/DVD movie, MPEG, AVI, DIVX, XVID, QuickTime or WMV

  • Films along with a completed entry form are to be submitted to Mr. Pope in Room 309

  • Deadline for submission: April 10th, 2008

Judging

All the films will be voted on after a final screening by a panel of judges, which will consist of teachers, former/current students and members of the film industry.
